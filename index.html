About us - So who are we?

Frank's Shop is a grassroots third party reseller of official merch from singer songwriter Frank Ocean, based out of the top floor of Fritzel's European Jazz Pub in New Orleans, USA. We chose this place as this pub was instrumental in Frank's inspiration to pursue music (plus the rent was pretty cheap).
We've been an independant reseller since 2016 when Frank released his groundbreaking and decade-defining album Blond(e), and launched his own merch line. We are seen as an alternative to other reseller sites like StockX or eBay.
We also donate a portion of all profits to charities and non-profit organisations in the area like Homeless America, the Hurricane Katrina fund and the LGBTQ+ mental health center.
If you have any questions or would like to help, you can contact us on our contact page.
